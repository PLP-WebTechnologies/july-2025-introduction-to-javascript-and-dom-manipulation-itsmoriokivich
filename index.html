Projects

Rooted In Style, Rooted in Nature initiative

This campaign invited people to embrace... style while actively supporting reforestation. We crafted a brand identity that linked every hoodie purchase to the planting of three trees, making the product both fashionable and purposeful. Through carefully designed visuals, slogans, and product presentations, the initiative gained a clear, memorable story that connected with audiences emotionally and practically. The result was a campaign that transformed everyday wear into a statement of environmental responsibility.

Noble Coffee Shop Branding

For Noble Coffee, a premium caf√© concept, we crafted... a complete brand identity that blends heritage with modern sophistication. The design system drew inspiration from the natural journey of coffee, from farm to cup. We combined deep, rich browns with accents of gold for a luxurious touch, and hints of red and green to reflect its organic roots. Our work spanned the full spectrum of branding: logo design (wordmark and logomark), brand patterns, packaging, signage, event collateral, and promotional posters. The result was a cohesive identity that feels both timeless and authentic, positioning Noble Coffee as more than just a coffee shop; but as an experience of craft, culture, and warmth. The launch was met with enthusiasm, and the brand now stands as a refined yet approachable symbol for coffee lovers.
